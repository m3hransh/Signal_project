\section{Convolution}
Convolution of signals $x_1[n]$ and $x_2[n]$ 
that symbolicly represented as $ x_1[n]*x_2[n]$ 
is as follow
\begin{align}
    y[n] = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{+\infty}x_1[k]x_2[n-k]
    \label{conv}
\end{align}
